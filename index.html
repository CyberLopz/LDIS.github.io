Common Identity Risks I Find

Most organizations don't discover these gaps until an account is compromised. A proactive assessment finds them first.

Admin accounts without phishing-resistant MFA — the single highest-risk configuration in any Microsoft 365 tenant
Legacy authentication protocols still enabled — bypasses all Conditional Access controls entirely
Overly permissive Conditional Access policies — gaps attackers probe for and exploit systematically
No Privileged Identity Management — standing admin access that should be just-in-time
Shared accounts and unreviewed service principals — orphaned access with no owner and no expiry
No risky sign-in monitoring or honeytoken alerting — active attacks occurring with no detection

Frequently Asked Questions

What size organizations do you work with?

Engagements range from small businesses (10–50 users) through mid-market organizations (500+ users). The sweet spot is organizations that have deployed Microsoft 365 but haven't had a dedicated security review of their identity and authentication stack.

Do you work with organizations outside Indiana?

Yes — all engagements are delivered remotely. LD Identity Security works with organizations anywhere in the United States. Indiana is home base, not a limitation.

Why are your prices higher than other consultants I've seen?

Lower-priced assessments typically produce templated reports without hands-on investigation. Every LD Identity Security engagement involves real technical work — actual review of your specific configuration, live hunting, and findings specific to your environment. You're paying for expertise, not a checklist.

Do you only work with Microsoft 365 environments?

The primary focus is the Microsoft security stack — Entra ID, Defender XDR, Intune, and M365. This specialization means deeper expertise than a generalist can offer in this specific environment.

Do you offer ongoing support after an assessment?

Yes. The monthly retainer is designed for organizations that want continuous identity security oversight — not just a one-time review. Retainer clients receive monthly reviews, alert monitoring, and ongoing advisory support.

Do you guarantee my organization won't be hacked?

No. Security assessments and hardening engagements are designed to significantly reduce risk and close known attack paths — not to provide absolute guarantees. Any consultant who promises otherwise isn't being honest with you.
